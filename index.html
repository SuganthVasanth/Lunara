Welcome to Lunara'24

Welcome to Lunara'24: Where innovation meets imagination! Join us for 11 electrifying events pushing boundaries and igniting creativity. Explore solutions, connect with our vibrant community, and let curiosity thrive at Lunara'24!

Lunara'24(Free the drive of life to explore) is a National Level Event which is one of the proud venture of our Department of Computer Science & Engineering. We aim at bringing out the technological and inventive skills from all the students irrespective of domains all over the country. It is our immense pleasure to invite each and every unique mind with magnificent ideas longing for a stage to exhibit out.
Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, an autonomous and self-financing institution, aspires to be an education center of excellence. With a commitment to global standards, the college emphasizes top-notch infrastructure, innovative teaching methods, advanced research facilities, and robust industry-academia collaborations. The mission is to transform students into intellectual, empathetic, and responsible citizens within an enterprising environment that values continual progress and diversity. The college aims to empower students to excel in a dynamically changing world.
